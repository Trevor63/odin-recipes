Delicious Mukimo Recipe

MUKIMO

Mukimo, mokimo or irio is a Kenyan meal (predominantly from communities living around Mount Kenya) prepared by mashing potatoes and green vegetables. It may also include maize and beans.

It is mostly served as an accompaniment for meat-based stew and nyama choma.

Ingredients

Behold; a list of ingredients used:

  • 4 large potatoes, chopped
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves crushed garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups of spinach or other leafy green, chopped
  • 500g frozen peas
  • 2 Tbsp fair trade olive oil

Directions

  1. Place potatoes, onions, garlic and salt in a pot with water to cover the tops of the potatoes.
  2. Bring to a boil then reduce to medium-high heat and simmer until potatoes are tender (about 20 minutes).
  3. Add spinach, corn and peas, and cook until water has completely evaporated (about 5 minutes).
  4. Add olive oil, salt and pepper to taste, and mix until spinach is tender.
  5. Remove from heat and mash entire mixture until it resembles mashed potatoes.
